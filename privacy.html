Privacy Policy

The Meal App · Effective date: June 27, 2026

The Meal App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use The Meal App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or precise location through the app.

Recipe and meal-planning data you create is stored on your device and/or in your personal iCloud account if iCloud sync is enabled by you.

How Your Information Is Used

Your data is used only to provide core app features such as saving recipes, planning meals, and syncing your content across your own Apple devices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple services (such as iCloud) to store or sync your content. Use of Apple services is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention

Data remains on your device and/or iCloud account until you delete it. If you uninstall the app, local data may be removed from your device.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your recipes and meal plans at any time in the app.
  • You can disable iCloud for the app in your iOS settings.

Children’s Privacy

The Meal App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
