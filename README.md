## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1) The app is not loading meals
- Check your internet connection.
- Force close and reopen the app.
- Make sure you are using the latest version of the app.

### 2) A recipe looks incorrect
We do our best to keep recipe information accurate. If something looks wrong, email us with the recipe name and details.

### 3) How do I report a bug?
Please email us with:
- Your device model
- iOS version
- App version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshot (if possible)
